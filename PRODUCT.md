# Product

## Register

product

## Users
- **Singers:** Choir members, often older adults, who need to view schedules, RSVP to events, and see where they are sitting. They require high legibility and simple, familiar mobile interfaces.
- **Admins:** Choir directors and managers who handle the logistics of the roster, attendance, and seating. They need efficient tools that work well on both mobile (for attendance) and desktop (for seating charts).

## Product Purpose
A lightweight, mobile-first management tool for non-profit choirs. It replaces manual spreadsheets and paper charts with a reliable digital system for attendance tracking, folder management, and automated seating chart generation.

## Brand Personality
- Easy to use
- Simple
- Familiar

## Anti-references
- **Aggressive Dark Mode:** Avoid "all black" themes or neon-on-dark styles.
- **Harsh Extremes:** Avoid vibrating or "harsh" contrast that strains the eye; favor soft, high-legibility neutrals.
- **Over-Complexity:** No hidden menus or non-standard UI patterns that might confuse less tech-savvy users.

## Design Principles
- **Frictionless Utility:** Prioritize the fastest path to common tasks like RSVPs and check-ins using standard, recognizable mobile patterns.
- **Soft Legibility:** Ensure high accessibility (WCAG) through generous spacing and clear typography rather than high-frequency color contrasts.
- **Reliable Stability:** Use a clean, structured layout that feels professional and stable, building trust with long-term institutional members.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Target:** WCAG 2.1 AA.
- **Needs:** Large tap targets (min 44px) for singers, high-contrast typography that avoids thin weights, and a layout that remains stable under browser zoom.
